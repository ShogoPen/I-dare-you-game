I dare you

a party game of nerve
Players (3-8)

How to play

1. The roll. Everyone rolls at once. Highest roll becomes the Darer, lowest becomes the Target. Ties re-roll among the tied players only. Whoever was Target last round is immune until every other player has had a turn.

2. The dare. The Darer draws the top card. The Target either completes it or plays a skip card, if they have one.

3. Skip cards. Earned by completing a Hard dare. Using one costs you 1 point.

4. Veto. Anyone can veto any drawn card, for free, at any time — no explanation needed, no arguments allowed. A vetoed card is discarded and a new one is drawn.

5. Winning. First player to reach 10 points wins.
